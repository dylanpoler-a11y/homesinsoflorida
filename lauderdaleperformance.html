What's in each revenue line

BucketWhat it includes
AccommodationPure room rate per night booked. Drives ADR and RevPAR (industry-standard).
CleaningCleaning fees collected from guest. Pass-through funding for housekeeping.
Other positiveResort/service fees, channel markup adjustments, Expedia Afe commission grossed back, pass-through taxes (state/county/other), extra-person fees, late check-out, damage charges, etc.
FeesNegative line items — Airbnb host service fee, Airbnb Resolution Center adjustments. These are deducted to compute Net to Host.
Gross RevenueSum of all positive line items — closest match to top-line revenue on the operator's P&L.
Net to HostGross Revenue minus channel/host fees.
Confidential — prepared by The Poler Team for active prospective buyers under NDA.
